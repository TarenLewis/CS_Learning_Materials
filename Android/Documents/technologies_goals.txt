#This is a doc to keep track of technologies that companies are interested in seeing experience with, in order
#to pursue internship & employment opportunities. It is a compilation of Kotlin and Android related job requirements 
# for internships and job postings which seem to be common among companies.

-Experience with Kotlin, and libraries such as RxJava and Retrofit
-Experience working with remote data via REST and JSON
-Experience with Android architecture and patterns such as MVVM
-Strong grasp of Git or other version control tools
-Have published at least one original Android app
-Experience with third-party libraries and APIs
-Solid understanding of the full mobile development life cycle
-Experience releasing apps on the App Store or Google Play
-Working knowledge of the general mobile landscape, architectures, trends, and emerging technologies
-Experience writing multi-threaded, asynchronous code.
-Experience working with backend server APIs.
-Solid UI development and unit testing skills.
-An understanding of how APIs work and how to integrate with them
-Understand RESTful API concepts and development.
-Familiarity with the Agile development process
-Knowledge about Mobile Broadband and Wi-Fi networks is a big plus
-You are fluent in at least two programming languages (preferably at least one that is strongly typed).
-You are genuinely passionate about technology. Maybe that comes in the form of keeping up to date with programming language and framework developers. 
----Or maybe you have a passion project of your own, whether that comes in the form of an open source project, community involvement and mentorship, 
----participating in meetups, or something else entirely.
-Knowledge and understanding of at least one native mobile language, eg. Java, Kotlin, Android Studio, Eclipse. etc
-Unit-test code for robustness, including edge cases, usability, and general reliability
-Ability to produce clean, bug-free, maintainable code
-Familiarity with architectural patterns such as MVVM, MVP
-Experience writing Bluetooth apps & familiarity with Android's Bluetooth SDK
-Experience using Map frameworks
-Knowledge of RxJava and/or Kotlin
-Strong UI development skills (complex view trees, animations, rendering and strong knowledge of the Android UI kit)
-Knowledgeable about test driven development (TDD), behavior driven development (BDD) and debugging.
-experience in popular frameworks such as Google Play services and Firebase
